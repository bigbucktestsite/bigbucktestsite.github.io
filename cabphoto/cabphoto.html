Photographs of a Big Buck Hunter arcade cabinet running the "Big Buck Hunter: Restoration" Mod

All images may be clicked to enlarge!

These are still images taken of the arcade machine running the mod. Big Buck Hunter: Restoration is not a video or collection of still images, it continually runs, generates new landscapes, and the animals inside react to their environment and to each other live. It never loops exactly; the same exact landscape is never made twice, and runs continually on a 3-day cycle of autumn, winter, and spring/summer.
